INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON FRONTIERS IN CHEMISTRY AND MATERIAL SCIENCE

(ICFCMS - 21)

21 - 22 December 2021

Organized by

DEPARTMENT OF CHEMISTRY

MANNAR THIRUMALAI NAICKER COLLEGE (AUTONOMOUS)

MADURAI, TAMIL NADU, INDIA

Profile of the Institution

The renowned educational organization ‘Mannar Thirumalai Naicker College’, Madurai has created a niche on the educational map of South India. It is a unique alma mater and has shaped a galaxy of eminent men and women shining in various realms of life in India and abroad. Our institution, established in the year 1974 is an epitome of education in the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu. The ‘Ipso Facto’ of the college is that on a gaining tempo in the years of its growth, it was reaccredited and was bestowed with the most coveted ‘A’ Grade by NAAC in cycle 3 in 2018. University Grants Commission has granted Autonomy to the institute in the academic year 2015-16.  It offers UG, PG, M.Phil., & Ph.D. programmes in various disciplines in both Aided and Self-Financing streams. University Grants Commission has recently sanctioned Rs.5 Crores, with the approval for the institution to launch 2 B.Voc, 6 Diploma programmes which are specially offered within the campus by the Community College, a unit of social dimension of our college. The institute is located at Pasumalai and has easy connectivity with the Airport (12Kms) and Madurai Railway Junction (6.5Kms).

About ICFCMS - 21

ICFCMS-21 covers a wide range of inter and multi-disciplinary themes in frontier areas of Chemistry and Material Science. It opens up a platform for researchers from National and International forum to present and discuss breaking novel research ideas. The theme of conference covers all broad areas in the field of Chemistry and Material Science. The Conference will feature talks by eminent resource persons, a number of plenary, invited talks and lectures focusing on different areas of Chemistry including Organic, Green Chemistry, Nanotechnology & Material Science. Besides there are several poster sessions. The best presentations will be specially awarded.

Thrust areas of the Conference

  • 1. Material Chemistry

  • 2. Organic Chemistry

  • 3. Inorganic Chemistry

  • 4. Physical Chemistry

  • 5. Industrial Chemistry

  • 6. Green Chemistry

  • 7. Medicinal Chemistry

  • 8. Nanotechnology

  • 9. Smart Materials

  • 10. Materials for Engineering Applications
